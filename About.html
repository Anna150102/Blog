My goal as a real estate coach with each of my programs is that my students have a paradigm shift where they will take the steps necessary for them to have a millionaire mindset & to make a decision today to become millionaires.

Believe it or not, I started my real estate journey in 2019. I saw a friend that was in real estate and was doing really well and I thought to myself, I'd better start researching this thing about investing in real estate. So, I started researching, reading books like Poor Dad Rich Dadd, Think and grow Rich, The Multifamily Millionaire, and many others. I started to join Real Estate Facebook Groups, listening to podcasts on wealth, on self development, and so on. I was thirsty for KNOWLEDGE and spent most of my free time on educating myself on real estate and how to invest in real estate. Then one day, I was feeling like it was not possible, and carried that belief that had been implanted by my self doubts for a coulple of days, then something funny happened, I heard an inner voice that said, you know what, just take a LEAP of faith!

And that was the moment that my mind started shifting to, it is possible, to then believing in it, to finally being in it! And that is when things really started happening for me! I started doing Joint Ventures on Flips, BRRRRs, Wholesaling, and just doing as many Flips and BRRRRs as possible, while also wholesaling properties. Through those JVs and through learning the art of wholesaling, I started making some serious money as I was all in on real estate.

At the same time, I was reading a lot of books on self development, like Bob Proctor's, Change Your Paradigm Change Your Life, or The Science of Getting Rich by Wallace D. Wattles, The Power of Awarness and the Wealth Mindset by Nevill Goddard, and so on. Looking back, I must have told my future self that I needed to learn everything I could on how to get rich. I was programming myselfto believe that I also could become rich through real estate. A big part of my coaching and how I differentiate myselffrom other real estate coaches is in instilling a believe in my students/mentees that they can accomplish anything!

Prior to being in real estate, I had worked for the biggest banks in Canada as a financial advisor, advising clients on how to save for retirement, how invest in stocks, mutual funds, options, derivatives, etc. Then in 2008 I had a spiritual awakening which took me toSouth Korea to teach english and travel around Asia. For about two years, I travelled throughout Asia, including to countries like< India, China, the Phillipines, etc. Those two years were essential for the awakening that we are creators of our reality. The reason Imention this is that the universe has a way of putting all your experiences together in the now and all the experiences that I have had and that you too have had and are having that point to the wisdom that we are powerful and have the ability to create our lives.

So, why coaching for me ...

The reason I started coaching was that I started seeing new real estate investors that were struggling with one aspect or another ofeal estate investing. So instead of not doing anything and continue to see them struggle, I decided that I could make a difference and help them in their real estate journey. I coach not because of the money, I coach because I am passionate about seeing others succeed. I enjoy seeing my students's or mentee's growth and see them achieve goals they never thought were possible.

I have all the tools, wisdom, knowledge, and resources to help you achieve amazing things in your real estate business and life!
